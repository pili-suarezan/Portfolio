Deepfake lab


#DeepLearning #Algorithms #DeepfakeSpotter #Manipulation


The paradigm of what is real or not regarding media is changing. Nowadays, we are used to encounter manipulated images and understand if they are edited. However, what about manipulated videos? What seems to be more real than deepfakes?

This project is about giving people access to practical knowledge about deepfakes and providing them with tools to recognize them. In order to achieve this, a website was created with some of our own experiments and examples.

Lumi


#InteractionDesign #ComplexSystem #ExperienceDesign


When someone says morning routine, the first thing you probably think about is “disaster” or “rush” and this gets worse if you have children to look after. Bad mood, unexpected events and fights are not the best way to start a day.

Lumi ensures that every day begins with happiness, positivity and energy. It consists of an interactive object for children to wake up in the morning in the best mood, reinforcing communication with parents and creating stronger bonds.

Digital twin manifesto


#ArtificialInteligence #Ethics #NearFuture


A digital twin is a digital replica of a living or non-living physical entity. Nowadays, our information is spread and shattered all over the net. What if we were able to collect and to be in control of our own information?

During the course of “Anthropology of Media”, we have created the Digital Twin Manifesto. It is a document, where we stipulate the basis and rules for the creation of a personal digital twin. Its purpose, is to protect our individual self and take responsibility within the evolution of technology.

Smylos


#Storytelling #GameDesign #InteractionDesign


We live in a world where social status is determined by money. There is a clear distinction between the ones that have and those who do not. Currency is a vital element of the everyday life as it sets the boundaries of what people can achieve. This raises the question: what if we redefine money?

Smylos is a hybrid board game that plays out in an ancient greek context, where wealth is determined by each person’s smile. The player starts his journey as an apprentice needing to track down an important potion. As the game progresses, he will face important choices that will make him reflect upon his values.
