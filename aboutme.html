ABOUT ME

Get to know me

Scroll down

Hi, my name is Pilar Suarez Anzorena. I am currently a student in the Laurea Magistrale in Communication Design at the Politecnico di Milano.

I was born and raised in Buenos Aires, where I graduated from the Universidad de Palermo in Industrial Design. During my degree, I had the opportunity of fulfilling an academic exchange in Valencia, Spain. This was one of the most enriching experiences of my life as it helped me grow professionally and personally. There, I discovered one of my deepest passions: getting to know new places and cultures.

Being the fourth of seven siblings, I deem teamwork and cooperation as core values. When working with others , I like to have a broad perspective of the process and attempt to nurture everyone’s strengths, while achieving a common goal. In my experience, I learnt that communication and dialogue are essential.

I define myself as a curious and observant person, always drawn to small details. Moreover, I tend to be a solution-oriented person, creating quick and effective solutions in critical moments. I like to be challenged and to push myself to learn new things, every experience is a growth opportunity.
