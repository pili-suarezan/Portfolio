The following projects were developed, given different themes, from the analysis of a defined problem, to the final design. The process included: market research of existent objects and the context of use, among others.

Packaging Design

Every assignment required a detailed imagery evaluation and a background analysis of each product. Different design techniques and decisions were taken to come up with a coherent and holistic design.

Don Ginés Chocolate


The aim of this project was to design the packaging for a new line of chocolates. These were thought to be minimalist, allowing the flavors to be easily identified and at the same time relating them as a line.

The images selected are simple and their shadows are repeated as a wake in different colors to make the composition dynamic and rhythmical.

Arroz Dacsa


The Basmati rice is harvested in the Himalayan mountainous zone. This is why for the redesign of its packaging we used the figure of a Buda, distinctive of the region. A medium tan tone cardboard is used, and the design is completed by a darker brown typography.

Industrial Design

The culmination of each project ended with usage and feasibility analysis, including a deep analysis in technical aspects such as: the selection of materials to fit the design, its production process and the projection of technical drawings for each component.

"Aria" foosball table


The “Aria” foosball table was designed for public spaces. This project encourages fun gatherings in parks. The materials used to develop this design were carefully chosen, bearing in mind the environment it would be exposed to. Therefore, it is resistant to different climate conditions.

"City" coffee machine


The “City” home capsule coffee machine was inspired by the rhythm of a fast based lifestyle metropoly. Coffee is essential for people to keep up with the city life. This idea is reflected through different textures, color palette and volumes.

"Reh" sink faucet


The “Reh” sink faucet design was inspired by nature. The organic shape transports the user to a rustic and natural atmosphere. It has two parts, the handle and the body. Both of them are made out of stainless steel for further resistance and durability.

Ultrasonic transducer


“Nection” is a wireless ultrasonic transducer, which means to improve the relationship between a doctor and its patients. It manages to reduce the psychological impact through its morphology. The transducer has an anatomical shape for the doctor to hold it comfortably and at the same time it is small, so the patient does not feel nervous about the procedure.
