SMYLOS

Interactive narrative

Scroll down

In the world of Smylos a person’s wealth defines its social status. Where usually wealth is defined by money, in Smylos it is defined by a smile. The bigger the smile, the wealthier you are. You cannot decide if you want to smile, it is just a measurement of your wealth. This project was developed as a group work during the Complex Artifacts and System Design Studio course.


Game

The interactive narrative is structured as a single-player hybrid board game. It consists of a board with a storyworld map printed on it, implemented with NFC tags and a mobile application. The narration is conveyed through an adventure in which the player has to accomplish a given mission.

Try the online prototype in this link: Smylos World

The player has to move his phone on the board to unlock and access the narrative contents available on the game app. The story is conveyed through two different media: written conversations and audios.

The written ones happen between the characters and the player, who has the possibility to interact and sometimes choose his own answer. They are presented as a common mobile chat. The player can also choose where to go and who to talk to, by moving the phone on the map.

The audios contain other conversations between the characters that the player can listen to but not answer. For further interaction, he is given the opportunity to eavesdrop on other characters talking privately with each other.

The game is meant to be sound-based as the characters are not visually defined, but rather depicted through their tone of voice and environment sounds.

Aim

The aim of the game is to find the ultimate potion which means a lot to some of the characters that appear during the adventure. Each one will try to influence the player to act on their behalf.

Game context

The game is to be placed in public spaces such as subways, airports and bus stations among others. By doing this, we take advantage of spaces that usually consist of a lot of waiting. With the technology implemented in the app, the player can play the game in one place and continue in another, making the experience even more fluid and immersive.

Starting point

The starting point for this project was the story of Crates from the book “Imaginary Lives". A story about a gifted and wealthy man who is inspired to abandon his fortune after he sees a king dressed as a homeless person in a theatre play. Crates decides to give away his talents and live in simplicity and poverty.

Characters creation

The characters of the game were created individually. Each of them was designed from scratch, including its background information and context, from the day they were born until the day they died. Through this method, we ensured we provided them with depth and detail. At last, the group created a relational map to link them with each other. Once their stories were intertwined, we assigned each character a specific role in the game plot.

City distribution

In the middle of the city stands a big tower with the office of Big Smile (the leader of the city). In its surroundings, we can find the business district, where the upper class runs its companies. On the ground floor of the tower, we encounter ourselves the city office, where every inhabitant needs to declare monthly his wealth in order to distribute the sizes of the smiles.

Going further from the centre to the edge of the city, people are progressively less wealthy. Outside the city, the slums spread out. There, people live in complete poverty, they don’t have a smile at all.

Between the city and the slum, there is a little shop where people from the slum go to get a potion to grow a temporary smile.

Game structure

The game is divided into four levels. In each one, the player can experience how the world is developing, respecting a certain order based on the characters he already met. The circle map represents how the conversations are unlocked at each level. Level 4 is a representation of the interaction between audio and responses the player can give since the whole level consists of different audios.
